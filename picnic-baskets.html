Picnic Basket Buying Guide

What to Consider:

  • Size & Capacity: Consider how many people you'll typically serve. Baskets range from intimate 2-person sets to larger 4-6 person options.
  • Insulation: Look for baskets with insulated compartments to keep food fresh and drinks cold during your outing.
  • Included Accessories: Quality baskets include plates, glasses, utensils, napkins, and often extras like cutting boards and wine openers.
  • Construction: Traditional wicker offers classic appeal, while modern materials like canvas provide durability and easy cleaning.
  • Portability: Consider weight and carrying options - some baskets include shoulder straps or handles for easier transport.

Our Top Recommendation: For most families, the Yellowstone Deluxe offers the best combination of quality, features, and value. Its premium construction and complete accessory set make it perfect for special occasions and regular use alike.
